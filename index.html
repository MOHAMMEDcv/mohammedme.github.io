Eng.Mohammed Alamarni

I am Mohammed Alamarni, a Computer Engineer with hands-on experience in Embedded Systems, UAV Technologies, Computer Vision, Networking, and practical engineering prototyping.

I have worked on several independent and freelance-style projects that combine hardware, software, sensors, automation, and real-world problem solving. My experience includes developing embedded systems, building Python-based applications, integrating cameras and sensors, and designing practical solutions for monitoring, automation, and data collection.

Technologies and tools I have worked with include:

  • ESP32
  • Arduino
  • Raspberry Pi
  • Python
  • OpenCV
  • YOLO
  • SQLite
  • I2C Communication
  • UART Communication
  • RS485 Communication
  • Modbus RTU over RS485
  • GPS and GSM Modules
  • Sensor Integration: GPS, IMU, BMP280, Magnetometer, and Camera Modules
  • Linux Basics
  • Computer Networking Fundamentals

My project experience includes GPS and GSM tracking systems, face recognition attendance systems using Raspberry Pi and OpenCV, warehouse automation and inventory tracking systems, YOLO-based object detection projects, ESP32-based control systems, sensor integration, TFT user interface design, and RS485-based pump control solutions.

I am passionate about building real-world engineering solutions that connect embedded hardware, software, AI, and automation. My goal is to continue growing as an Embedded Systems Engineer while developing reliable systems that solve practical problems.
